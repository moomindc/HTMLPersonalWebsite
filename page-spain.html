Areas we're looking at

Cádiz province

Top of the list. The coast between Tarifa and Sanlúcar is excellent. Prices still reasonable. Wind can be strong on the coast — factor this in for any property with outdoor space.

Málaga province

More expensive than Cádiz but infrastructure is better and the international community larger. Looking at towns inland from the coast rather than the coast itself.

Almería coast

Underrated. Driest climate in Europe. Property prices significantly lower. Less infrastructure but that may change. Keeping it on the watchlist.

Seville (city)

Not coastal but very liveable. Strong cultural life, good transport links, reasonable cost of living for a Spanish city. Would suit if we want more urban life.
